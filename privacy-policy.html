Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

🔒 Your data never leaves your device. Smart Loan Advisor works entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Smart Loan Advisor does not collect any personal information. All data you enter — including loan amounts, interest rates, income, and financial details — is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server or third party.

2. How Your Data Is Used

All calculations and financial analysis are performed entirely on your device. Your data is used solely to provide you with loan calculations, comparisons, and financial insights within the app.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This data:

  • Remains on your device at all times
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Can be deleted at any time by clearing the app data
  • Is not shared with any third parties

4. In-App Purchases

Smart Loan Advisor offers a one-time premium upgrade ($3.99) processed through Google Play Billing. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not collect or store any payment information.

5. Internet Access

The app may request internet access solely for the purpose of processing in-app purchases through Google Play. No personal or financial data entered in the app is transmitted over the internet.

6. Third-Party Services

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not integrate with any third-party SDKs that collect data.

7. Children's Privacy

Smart Loan Advisor is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
